About SCDC

Everything mutes where the legacy speak. Having a history of 20+ years in the field of education, Sree Chaitanya has a record of achieving state level ranks in intermediate level. The vision has been amplified into broader range and laid a founding stone of Sree Chaitanya Degree College for graduate education in the academic year 2016-17, under the same proven management. Sree Chaitanya Degree College possesses well qualified and experienced staff in the field of teaching. At Sree Chaitanya, we believe in authorizing access to lecturers for students at any time to encourage the curiosity, as we mark it as an important characteristic of learning behavior. The buildings are equipped with well-furnished laboratories and spacious digital classrooms. Every classroom has limited seats to take special care of every student. Our values at fostering quality education with corporate standards will stand at top notch right from administration to teaching level. The vision of correspondent to give back to society fills the soul of motivation of the organization.
